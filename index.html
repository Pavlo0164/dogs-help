About us

PetSafety not only aids in reuniting lost pets with their owners but also plays a crucial role in providing shelter for animals in need.

Our commitment extends beyond the swift reunification process, as we actively work to secure safe havens for those who require them. The heartwarming stories of successful reunions we've facilitated stand as a testament to the compassion and generosity of the community.

Furthermore, our platform serves as a beacon of hope for those currently facing the distressing situation of a missing pet. The support we offer goes beyond mere technological assistance; it embodies the collective spirit of animal lovers and caring individuals who rally together for a common cause. We take pride in not only reuniting families but also in finding loving homes for animals that, through our efforts, have been rescued from uncertainty.

At PetSafety, we believe in the power of community and the positive impact it can have on both pets and their owners. If you find yourself in the unfortunate situation of a lost pet, trust us to be your ally in the journey to bring them back home.

Happy stories

One day, during our routine stroll, our hearts sank as we discovered Max had gone missing. Panic set in as we tirelessly searched every nook, calling out his name. Realizing the urgency of the situation, we turned to a PetSafety website, hoping that someone in our community had seen our beloved Max.

To our immense relief, we found a post from neighbor who had found Max and taken him in. The neighbor detailed the discovery, describing the location where Max was found and providing contact information. As we approached the neighbor's house, the door opened, revealing Max's wagging tail and joyful bark.

The entire episode highlighted the power of community and technology working hand in hand. Max's adventure, from the moment he went missing to the heartwarming reunion, became a testament to the strength of compassion and the bonds that connect us all.
